Introduction to Stylesheets

Where it started

Competition among browser manufacturers drove them to introduce more and more features into HTML until page sources were hopelessly cluttered and often ran properly on just one manufacturer's browser.

What came next

The industry realised that the complexity had become bad for business and they agreed to create a common standard for enhanced HTML.
